Privacy Policy

As of: February 2026

1. Introduction

The protection of your personal data is important to us. This privacy policy informs you about which data is collected when using the app «SetupKart Pro» and how it is used.

Responsible for data processing: Hermann Kopp, Switzerland (email: appstract7@gmail.com).

2. Principle: Local Data Storage

SetupKart Pro was developed following the «Privacy by Design» principle. All setup data you enter is stored locally on your device by default.

  • No personal data transmitted to external servers (except optional weather query, see 3.6)
  • No user account required
  • No tracking tools used
  • No analytics or advertising services integrated

If the user activates future optional cloud features, data may be stored on external servers. In this case, the user will be informed in advance and the privacy policies of the respective cloud provider will also apply.

3. What data is processed?

3.1 Setup data (local)
All technical data you enter in the app is stored exclusively on your device and is not transmitted to the provider.

3.2 GPS location (optional)
The app can optionally use your GPS location to automatically identify the race track. This data is processed locally only and not shared with third parties (except for the optional weather query, see 3.6).

3.3 Voice input (optional)
The voice input function uses your device's speech recognition. Processing is subject to your device manufacturer's privacy policy (Google/Android).

3.4 Device ID (for licensing)
A device ID is used to bind the activation code to your device. This is stored exclusively for licence management purposes.

3.5 Contact data (for purchase)
Your email address is used to deliver the activation code and is not shared with third parties.

3.6 Weather data (optional)
The app can optionally fetch current weather data via the Open-Meteo API. GPS coordinates (latitude, longitude) are transmitted to the service open-meteo.com. No personal data is transmitted. Open-Meteo is an open-source weather service that requires no registration. More information: open-meteo.com.

4. Setup Export and Sharing

The function for exporting and sharing setup data (.kart files) is initiated by the user. The provider has no influence on who the user shares data with.

5. Google Play Store

If you obtain the app via the Google Play Store, Google's privacy policy also applies. Google may collect usage data (e.g. downloads, crashes). The provider has no influence on this.

6. User Rights

You have the right to information, correction, deletion, restriction of processing and data portability at any time. Contact: appstract7@gmail.com.

7. Data Security

Local storage provides a high level of data security as no transmission over the internet takes place (except for the optional weather query).

8. Retention Period

Locally stored data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app. Contact and licence data is stored in accordance with statutory retention periods.

The provider reserves the right to amend this privacy policy as needed. The current version is always available at setupkart.ch.
